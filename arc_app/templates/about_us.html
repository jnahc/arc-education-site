Academy Revolutionary Confideration

ARC.com is an online learning platform aimed at professional adults and students. ARC, a portmanteau of you + academy, has more than 30 million students and 50,000 instructors teaching courses in over 60 languages. There have been over 245 million course enrollments. Students and instructors come from 190+ countries and 2/3 of students are located outside of the U.S. ARC also has over 4,000 enterprise customers and 80% of Fortune 100 companies use ARC for employee upskilling (ARC for Business). Students take courses largely as a means of improving job-related skills. Some courses generate credit toward technical certification. ARC has made a special effort to attract corporate trainers seeking to create coursework for employees of their company.
